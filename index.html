General Overview

When I was a kid I traded my special foldable NERF gun for my neighbors DVD of what would become my favorite movie of all time. I would watch it over and over again for months. As I got older I forogt why I was so obsessed with it as a kid. I would, on a whim, rewatch it for nostalgias sake and came to an important conclusion. Speed Racer(2008) is one of the greatest movies ever made.

Made by the Wachowskis Sisters of Matrix fame, Speed Racer is a live action interpretation of the classic sixties anime. An action filled high speed adventure that shares a surprisingly heartfelt and nuanced story. This story is presented with some of the most engaging cinematography and charming scores in film.
