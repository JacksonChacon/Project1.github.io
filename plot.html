But many still find themselves unfamiliar with Speed Racers general plot, especially with how they reinterpreted it for film.

The story follows the Racer family with parents Ma and Pop, eldest son Rex, middle son Speed, youngest son Sprittle, and pet chimpanze Chim-Chim. The family runs their own autoshop/racing team and all of suns want to be racecar drivers. After Rex Racer's death however their is an air of occasional pause. Nonetheless Speed becomes one of the greatest up and coming racers in the world, enough so to catch the attention of Royalton Industries. Royalton is the Microsoft of racing and they want Speed to represent them during his future races. The issue is that Royalton wants exclusivity even from the Racers brand. Speed ends up choosing family over Royalton who responds by sabotaging Speed's next race, destroying the Mach 5(Speeds car), and serving the Racers with bogus lawsuits. After a handful of racing related shenanigans Speed is able to win the biggets race on his own merit, exposing Royalton in the process, and declaring the importance of artistic dedication over consumerist corruption.
