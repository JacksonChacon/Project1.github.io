Made by the ever excellent Michael Giacchino, as revered for soundtracks as the Wachowskis are for films, Speed Racer's score is a sound to behold. Take a listen to some of my favorites from the film.

These are in no way Michael Giacchino's best works (The Incredibles, Rogue One) but I enjoy the soundtrack nonetheless.
